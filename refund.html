Refund Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026.

Loomstate offers a fixed-fee consulting engagement (the Founder Agent-Ops Audit) at USD 750 per engagement. This Refund Policy describes when and how refunds are issued.

1. Pre-kickoff full refund window

If you request a refund before the kickoff call has occurred, Loomstate will issue a full refund of USD 750 within 7 business days. Email loomstate.official@gmail.com to request the refund. No reason is required.

2. After kickoff, before deliverables

If the kickoff call has occurred but no deliverables have been sent, Loomstate will issue a partial refund of USD 600 (USD 150 retained for kickoff time and initial discovery), within 7 business days of the request.

3. After deliverables sent

Once Loomstate has sent any of the documented deliverables (reliability scorecard, failure-pattern checklist, repo-specific operating checklist, action plan), the engagement is considered substantially complete and the fee is non-refundable. If you believe the deliverables fall materially short of the documented scope, email Loomstate within 14 days of delivery; the operator will review the concern in good faith and, where the gap is clear, either correct the deliverables at no extra charge or issue a discretionary partial refund. This is in addition to your statutory rights, which are not waived.

4. How refunds are issued

Refunds are issued via Stripe to the original payment method. Stripe's processing fees are absorbed by Loomstate. Refunds typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days, depending on your card issuer.

5. Chargebacks

If you have a concern, please email Loomstate first. The operator answers within one business day and will work in good faith to resolve issues before a chargeback is necessary. Chargebacks filed without prior contact may delay resolution.

6. Disputes

Disputes about refund eligibility are governed by the Terms of Service.

7. Contact

Refund requests and questions: loomstate.official@gmail.com.
