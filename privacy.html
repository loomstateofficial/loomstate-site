Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Loomstate collects, how it is used, and your choices. Loomstate is a solo-operator service. By engaging Loomstate, contacting the operator, or using this website, you accept the practices described below.

1. Information collected

Loomstate collects only the information necessary to deliver the Founder Agent-Ops Audit and operate this website:

  • Contact information — your name, email address, and any business or repository details you share by email or during the engagement.
  • Engagement materials — repository contents, agent transcripts, prompts, and any other materials you provide for the audit.
  • Payment information — processed by Stripe. Loomstate does not store or have access to your full payment card details. Stripe's privacy practices apply to that processing; see stripe.com/privacy.
  • Basic web logs — if this site is hosted on a third-party platform (e.g., GitHub Pages), the host may log standard request data such as IP address, user agent, and timestamps. Loomstate does not run analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising scripts on this site.

2. How information is used

Information you provide is used to deliver the engagement, communicate with you, send invoices and receipts, and meet legal or tax obligations. Loomstate does not sell your information, share it with advertisers, or use it for purposes other than those described here.

3. Confidentiality

Engagement materials are treated as confidential. Loomstate will not disclose your repository contents, prompts, or transcripts to third parties without your prior written consent, except as required by law or as needed to operate Loomstate's own infrastructure (e.g., backup of working notes on operator-controlled storage).

4. Storage and retention

Engagement materials are stored on operator-controlled devices and operator-controlled cloud accounts (e.g., GitHub, encrypted local storage). Loomstate retains engagement materials for up to 12 months after delivery for support and reference purposes, then deletes them on request or at retention end. You may request deletion at any time by emailing the address below.

5. Subprocessors

Loomstate uses the following third-party services in the normal course of operating the business: Stripe (payment processing), Google (email and calendar via the operator's Loomstate Gmail account), and the website host (GitHub Pages or equivalent static host). These providers process limited data on Loomstate's behalf and are subject to their own privacy policies.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information Loomstate holds about you, and to object to or restrict processing. To exercise these rights, email the contact address below. Loomstate will respond within a reasonable time and at no charge for routine requests.

7. Security

Loomstate uses industry-standard practices to protect engagement materials in transit and at rest, including encrypted storage on operator-controlled devices and access-controlled accounts. No system is perfectly secure; if Loomstate discovers a breach affecting your data, it will notify you without undue delay.

8. Cookies and tracking

This website does not set cookies, run analytics, or use tracking pixels. The host platform may set technical cookies required for site operation; those are governed by the host's policy.

9. Children

Loomstate's services are intended for adults operating businesses. Loomstate does not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

10. Changes

Loomstate may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always posted at this URL with the effective date above. Material changes will be communicated to active clients in writing.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: loomstate.official@gmail.com.
